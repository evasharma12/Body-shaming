I was sad, and I believed that there was something very wrong with me.”

“For years I have been passionate about exercise and fitness, but I felt uncomfortable talking about it. I have a tall, slender build and I was nervous about being judged. – Dr. Nina Watson

“After five years of dating, my boyfriend told me he did not want to date me anymore because I was fat. – Leticia A. Mathis
